    Know More About ITI Course !


ITI courses after the 10th are designed to provide students with specialized skills and knowledge in the IT sector.

courses range from certificate courses to diploma courses and cover a wide range of subjects, such as programming languages, web designing, networking, hardware, and software maintenance.

Eligibility Criteria For ITI Course


A 10th pass or equivalent is the minimum eligibility required to obtain admission in ITI. The duration may vary from one year to 3 years depending upon the course chosen. The age limit is between 14-40 years.
