BRANCHES IN ENGINEERING

Engineering encompasses a vast array of disciplines focused on innovation and problem-solving. From mechanical and civil to electrical, chemical, and aerospace, engineers drive technological advancement across various fields. They design machines, shape infrastructure, power technology, manage processes, and pioneer flight, shaping the world we live in.There are various branches in engineering.Engineering course duration is 4 years with 6 months of internship.After Engineering BTech degree is awarded to students who have completed their studies and training in their taken field.

Exams Required for engineering:

1.The Common Entrance Test (CET) is a competitive exam conducted for the purpose of admission of students to the first year or first semester of full-time courses in engineering courses in professional colleges the various states of India.

CET website

2.The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is a national-level engineering entrance examination which is conducted for admission to undergraduate courses in India. This exam provides a common platform for aspiring candidates to become eligible for admission to top engineering colleges through a common entrance examination.

JEE website

After Engineering exams for MS/Mtech:

1.CAT or Common Admission Test is a computer-based test for admission to graduate management programs. The test is conducted for admissions to MBA/PGDBM programs at IIMs and more than 1,200 other B-schools in India.

CAT website

2.The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an entrance examination conducted in India that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of undergraduate subjects in engineering and sciences for admission into postgraduate programs.

GATE website
