    Know More About Paramedical Course !


Paramedical courses focus on theoretical knowledge and practical training for professions like Nursing, Physiotherapy, Radiology, Optometry, Medical Laboratory Technology, etc.

These professionals provide support and assistance to medical staff, like doctors.

Eligibility Criteria For Paramedical Course


To be eligible for paramedical courses after 10th students must pass class 10. They must have Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as their core subjects.

Diploma & certificate courses in paramedical do not require students to qualify for any entrance exam.
